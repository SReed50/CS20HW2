About Us

Our Mission

At Dunder Mifflin, we pride ourselves on customer service and our relationships that we build with our family of clients. We provide a level of service that big paper suppliers simply cannot match. Our company services the Northeast region of the U.S. through our headquarters in New York City and our regional branches located in Albany (NY), Binghamton (NY), Buffalo (NY), Camden (NJ), Nashua (NH), Pittsfield (MA), Scranton (PA), Stamford (CT), Utica (NY), and Yonkers (NY).
